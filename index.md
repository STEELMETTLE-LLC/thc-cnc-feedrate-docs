## What To Expect

- Values are starting points only.
- Feedrate, pierce timing, and height settings require on-machine tuning.
- Material lot, air quality, consumable condition, and table behavior affect final values.

## Quick Tuning Order

1. Tune feedrate.
2. Tune pierce time.
3. Tune pierce and cut height.
4. Re-verify with corners and fine features.

## Notice

Always validate with test coupons before production runs.
